Bike Sharing Market

Welcome to the bike sharing market information page. Here you can take a look at the bike sharing market.

Bike sharing is a shared transportation service in which conventional bikes or electric bikes are made available for shared use to individuals for a short-term basis for a price or free.

Users are able to pick up bicycles around the city from multiple docked or dock-less stations and return it at another same system.

By 2022, approximately 3,000 cities worldwide offer bike-sharing systems.
