Map

This is a map on Citibike stations in New York City
You can click on each station below to get data
It is also categorized into five types of stations:
1. Coming soon (Colored in Yellow)
2. Empty (Colored in Red)
3. Low (Number of bikes less than 5) (Colored in Orange)
4. Normal (Colored in Green)
5. Out of order (Colored in Dark Blue)
You can use the tool on the top right of the map to select what category of stations you are looking for.
The summary table provided on the bottom right gives you a real time update on the number of stations.
